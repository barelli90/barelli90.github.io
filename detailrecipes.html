Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 600g savoy cabbage
  • 150ml unsweetened almond milk
  • 3 tablespoons light olive oil
  • 30g flaked almonds, toasted
  • 4 sprigs tarragon, leaves roughly chopped

Total time required 10 mins

  • Cooking time: 10 mins

Instructions

  1. Separate the cabbage leaves, halving and removing the tough rib in the centre of the leaf as you go.
  2. Place a large saucepan on a hob at a high temperature. Add the olive oil and wait for thirty seconds for it to warm up. Add the cabbage leaves and cook for 4 mins, pushing around occasionally (this will be easiest with tongs), until the majority of the leaves appear vibrant in colour, glossy and partially wilted.
  3. Pour in the almond milk â it should bubble up immediately if your pan is at the right temperatureâ then add the tarragon, toasted almonds and a good pinch of sea salt and lots and lots of black pepper.
  4. Cook for 45-60 secs more, moving all the time, and decant into a serving bowl before the cabbage turns translucent and soggy.
